About Donsol

The Municipality of Donsol, located in Sorsogon Province, Philippines, is a leading eco-tourism destination internationally known for its sustainable whale shark (butanding) interaction program. Guided by environmental protection and community participation, Donsol promotes responsible tourism that supports conservation and local livelihoods.

Mission

We advocate intense implementation of Agri-Tourism guidelines and develop world-class tourism facilities, provide effective delivery of basic services, provide sustainable development, enhance people’s participation, judicious utilization of resources and committed governance towards the realization of our vision.

Vision

A premiere agri-tourism destination in Bicol where God-loving, disaster resilient and adaptive communities, thriving in a safe and orderly built and natural environment and a progressive economy under an inclusive governance system
